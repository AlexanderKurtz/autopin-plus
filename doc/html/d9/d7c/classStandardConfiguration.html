void StandardConfiguration::getArgs ( QString  arg) [private]

Simple method for parsing configuration options.

Takes a string of the form "var=val", "var+=val1 val2" or "var-=val1 val2" and sets var to val, adds val1, val2 to var or removes val1, val2 from var. Leading and trailing whitespace in arg and val will be removed.

Parameters:
[in]argString of the form "var=val", "var+=val1 val2" or "var-=val1 val2"
